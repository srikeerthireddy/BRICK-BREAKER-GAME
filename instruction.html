Controls:

Left Arrow: Move the paddle left.

Right Arrow: Move the paddle right.

Have fun and good luck!

Game Play :

=> Start : Press the spacebar to launch the the ball and begin the game.

=>Paddle Movement: Use the left and right arrow keys to move the paddle.

=> Ball Movement: The ball will bounce off the paddle and walls.Keep it from falling to the bottom.

=> Brick Breaking: Hit the bricks with the ball to break them.

Winning: Clear all the blocks

Scoring:

Points are awarded for each brick broken.

For each brick the score increases to 100.

Game Over:

When the ball touches the bottom of the screen of not balancing with the paddle the game ends.

Click on the play Again button you can restart the game.
